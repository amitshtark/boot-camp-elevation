Tomato Pasta Recipe

A simple and tasty pasta recipe with tomato sauce, garlic, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add the pasta and cook until soft.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  4. Add garlic and tomato sauce.
  5. Mix the pasta with the sauce.
  6. Add cheese on top and serve.
